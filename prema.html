What is Prema?

Prema or Bhakti means the highest form of the love of God in Hinduism.

Bhakti is one of the central concepts of Hinduism

Now how to practise Prema?
• Chant and praise the divine
• Study sacred scriptures
• Have faith in the divine and be humble
• Appreciate the wonder and beauty of nature
