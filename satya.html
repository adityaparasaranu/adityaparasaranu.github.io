What is Satya?

“God is only truth & Truth is only god”

Satya means truth. Satya is a central theme in the Vedas. Satya is important for Ṛta, which is the principle of natural order which regulates and coordinates the operation of the universe and everything within it.

Being honest with yourself and others. It's about being honest with yourself, honest with others, and refraining from judgment — making sure that you speak and act with thought and intention (not just saying whatever is on your mind).
