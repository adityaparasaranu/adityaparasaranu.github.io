What is Brahmacharya?

Brahmacharya is when a person completely controls his body and mind and completely avoids indulgences through self-discipline

We can practice Brahmacharya by,

• Avoiding all earthly indulgences
• Listen to our body
• Be in silence once a day for 5-10 minutes.
• Doing daily meditation and constant japa
