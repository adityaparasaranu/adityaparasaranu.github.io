What is Shanti?

Shanti means peace or inner peace.

We can practice Shanti by,

• Meditate
• Be Grateful.
• Take Responsibility for Your Actions.
• Don't Let Your Past Mistakes Define You.
• Love Yourself.
• Practice Acceptance and Contentment.
